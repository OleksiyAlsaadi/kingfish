Pleasant Valley Mechatronics - 100% Student Run
(From Left to Right: Ian McNannie, Alex Elson, Weston Cramer)



        Pleasant Valley Mechatronics is a student run organization formed from a common

interest in robotics, engineering, and programming. Our first formal introduction occurred

in 2013, leading to PV's first mechatronics club. As a result, we take pride in helping

our school delve into the more technological side of the twenty-first century.
